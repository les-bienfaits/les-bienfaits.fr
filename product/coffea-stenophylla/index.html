Coffea stenophylla, also known as highland coffee of Sierra Leone is a species of Coffea originating from West Africa. It is native to the West African countries of Guinea, Ivory Coast, Liberia and Sierra Leone. The plant grows as a shrub or tree, to a height of up to 20 feet. Ripe C. stenophylla berries are a dark purple, in contrast to C. arabica, whose berries turn red when ripe.
